About server

Kuudere's bunnyhop - is a new server, there are 1090 zoned maps. We use shavit's bhoptimer and many other plugins that will be useful to you (check command section).

Styles

  1. Normal - Basic autobhop style
  2. Sideways - Just sideways
  3. Half-Sideways - Jump with the W key held down
  4. Backwards - Just backwards
  5. W-Only - You can only use the W key
  6. A/D-Only - You can only use the A or D key
  7. S-Only - You can only use the S key
  8. Scroll - Jump only on scroll
  9. 400 Velocity - Speed limit at 400 Velocity
  10. 600 Velocity - Speed limit at 600 Velocity
  11. 3k Velocity - Speed limit at 3000 Velocity
  12. Low Gravity - Just low gravity
  13. Jump Impulse - High jump height
  14. Segmented - You can save your time, speed and position using the menu
  15. Segmented LG - The same as LG, but with segments menu
  16. Auto-Strafe - Normal with autostrafing
  17. Prespeed - You can pick up more speed at the start
  18. TAS - Autostrafe, segments, timescale and others
  19. LG TAS - The same as LG, but with tas
  20. JI TAS - The same as JI, but with tas
  21. 3k Vel TAS - The same as 3k Velocity, but with tas
  22. Super TAS - The same as TAS, but less speed loss
  23. Stamina - Jump with stamina
  24. Hard Stamina - Jump with stamina, height lowered (0.9)
  25. Insane Stamina - Jump, with stamina height lowered (0.8)
  26. Impossible Stamina - Jump, with stamina height lowered (0.7)
  27. Seg Hard Stamina - Hard Stamina with segments
  28. Speedrun - You get more speed after each jump
  29. Speedrun TAS - The same as Speedrun, but with TAS menu
  30. Half-Life 2 - Experience close to HL2, scroll, Accelerated Back Hopping
  31. HL2 TAS - Half-Life 2 with TAS
  32. Slow Motion - Normal in slow motion
  33. Unreal - You can push off the walls by shooting at them, you also have a double jump
  34. UntealSEG - Unreal with segments menu
  35. NOOB - Normal with autostrafe and no speed lose

Commands

  1. !help - How to jump
  2. !style - Choose your bhop style
  3. !r - Start/restart your timer
  4. !b - Start your timer on the bonus track
  5. !m - Start your timer on the main track
  6. !end - Teleport to endzone
  7. !pause - Toggle pause
  8. !hud - Opens the HUD settings menu
  9. !js - Opens the SSJ, JHUD and other settings menu
  10. !spec - Moves you to the spectators' team
  11. !hide - Toggle players' hiding
  12. !goto - Teleport to another player
  13. !tier - Prints the map's tier to chat
  14. !rank - Show your or someone else's rank
  15. !top - Show the top 100 players
  16. !stats - Show the player's profile
  17. !replay - Opens the replay bot menu
  18. !myreplay - Opens your replay menu
  19. !preview - Preview your unfinished replay
  20. !wr - View the leaderboard of a map
  21. !rr - View the recent #1 times set
  22. !nominate - Nominate map for !rtv
  23. !rtv - Vote for change the map
  24. !noclipme - Toggle noclip
  25. !noclipspeed - Change speed of noclip
  26. !ssjtop - Show the top SSJ players
  27. !ljtop - Show the top LongJump players
  28. !ljsettings - Toggle LongJump stats
  29. !sound - Stop map music
  30. !noslide - Stops sliding on easybhop styles upon landing
  31. !scrollhud - Toggle HUD for scroll
  32. !perf - Play sound for perfect jump in scroll
  33. !line - Show the route of the WR
  34. !edgehelper - Will show the edges of your hitbox if you stand on the edge
  35. !groundticks - Will show the time spent on the ground before jumping
  36. !jumpcircle - Will show the approximate distance of your jump
  37. !showclips - Show clips on map
  38. !showtriggers - Show triggers on map
  39. !sts - Open triggers settings
  40. !yawdiff - Show your yaw
  41. !dist - Place two points and measure the distance between them

Known Issues

  1. Plugin line (!line) may not work correctly.
  2. Bonus zones are placed incorrectly on the bhop_arcane_v2 (automatically by [shavit] Map Zones (JSON))
