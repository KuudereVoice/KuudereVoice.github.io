About server

Kuudere bunnyhop - is a new server. We use shavit's bhoptimer and many other plugins that will be useful to you (check command section). There are 730 zoned maps.

Styles

  1. Normal - Basic autobhop style
  2. Sideways - Just sideways
  3. Half-Sideways - Jump with the W key held down
  4. Backwards - Just backwards
  5. W-Only - You can only use the W key
  6. A/D-Only - You can only use the A or D key
  7. S-Only - You can only use the S key
  8. Scroll - Jump only on scroll
  9. 400 Velocity - Speed limit at 400 Velocity
  10. Low Gravity - Just low gravity
  11. Jump Impulse - High jump height
  12. Segmented - You can save your time, speed and position using the menu
  13. Segmented LG - The same as LG, but with segments menu
  14. Autostrafe - Normal with autostrafing
  15. PRE - You can pick up more speed at the start
  16. TAS - Autostrafe, segments, timescale and others
  17. LG TAS - The same as LG, but with tas
  18. Super TAS - The same as TAS, but less speed loss
  19. Stamina - Jump height lowered (0.9)
  20. Hard Stamina - Jump height lowered (0.8)
  21. Insane Stamina - Jump height lowered (0.7)
  22. Segmented Hard Stamina - Hard Stamina with segments
  23. Speedrun - You get more speed after each jump
  24. Speedrun TAS - The same as Speedrun, but with TAS menu
  25. Half-Life 2 - Experience close to HL2, scroll, Accelerated Back Hopping
  26. Half-Life 2 TAS - HL2 with TAS
  27. Half-Life 2 Old engine - Forward scroll, boost after jump
  28. Half-Life 2 Old engine TAS - HL2 Old engine with TAS
  29. Slow Motion - Normal in slow motion
  30. Unreal - You can push off the walls by shooting at them, you also have a double jump
  31. UntealSEG - Unreal with segments menu
  32. NOOB - Normal with autostrafe and no speed lose

Commands

  1. !style - Choose your bhop style
  2. !r - Start/restart your timer
  3. !b - Start your timer on the bonus track
  4. !m - Start your timer on the main track
  5. !end - Teleport to endzone
  6. !pause - Toggle pause
  7. !hud - Opens the HUD settings menu
  8. !js - Opens the SSJ, JHUD and other settings menu
  9. !spec - Moves you to the spectators' team
  10. !hide - Toggle players' hiding
  11. !goto - Teleport to another player
  12. !tier - Prints the map's tier to chat
  13. !rank - Show your or someone else's rank
  14. !top - Show the top 100 players
  15. !stats - Show the player's profile
  16. !replay - Opens the replay bot menu
  17. !wr - View the leaderboard of a map
  18. !rr - View the recent #1 times set
  19. !nominate - Nominate map for !rtv
  20. !rtv - Vote for change the map
  21. !noclipme - Toggle noclip
  22. !noclipspeed - Change speed of noclip
  23. !ssjtop - Show the top SSJ players
  24. !ljtop - Show the top LongJump players
  25. !ljsettings - Toggle LongJump stats
  26. !scrollhud - Toggle HUD for scroll
  27. !perf - Play sound for perfect jump in scroll
  28. !line - Show the route of the WR
  29. !edgehelper - Will show the edges of your hitbox if you stand on the edge
  30. !groundticks - Will show the time spent on the ground before jumping
  31. !jumpcircle - Will show the approximate distance of your jump
  32. !showclips - Show clips on map
  33. !showtriggers - Show triggers on map
